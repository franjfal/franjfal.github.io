Geometría y holomorfía en dimensión finita e infinita

2017-2025

About the seminar

The seminar is considered in the line of a working group where the speaker is free to talk about any project he/she is interested in. Presentations can consist of new results, classical ones or a mix of both. The emphasis on the details and long explanations of the proofs are welcome.
