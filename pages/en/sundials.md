# The mathematics of time

<iframe src="https://www.google.com/maps/d/embed?mid=1oK3DkfY6cYyLDsOH4XlyyAYrwu-IoTf6&ehbc=2E312F" width="640" height="480"></iframe>

The route includes the sundials at:

* Meteorological Centre
* Valencia Cathedral, La Seu
* Church of Sant Joan del Mercat
* Church of Sant Tomas i Sant Felip Neri
* Astronomy Garden - City of Arts and Sciences

Other interesting sundials in the city of Valencia can also be found at:

* Campanar Neighbourhood Association
* C. del Mediterranio, 13
* Casa dels Bous
* Church of El Pilar, Valencia
* Church of El Patriarca
