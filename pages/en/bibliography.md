# Books

* [Change Is the Only Constant: The Wisdom of Calculus in a Madcap World](https://www.goodreads.com/en/book/show/43885052){:target="_blank"}. **Orlin, Ben**, Black Dog & Leventhal, 2019. A book in which each chapter explains a concept from mathematical analysis in an engaging way. Although the tone is informal, some background knowledge is recommended.
* [El libro de las matemáticas](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/15994871-el-libro-de-las-matem-ticas-de-pit-goras-a-la-57-dimensi-n-250-hitos?from_search=true&from_srp=true&qid=YJinYN0nK9&rank=1){:target="_blank"}. **A. Pickover, Cliffor**, ILUS BOOKS, 2014. A book that presents mathematical concepts in chronological order, each accompanied by an illustration.
* [En busca del grafo perdido: Matemáticas con puntos y rayas](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/59776447-en-busca-del-grafo-perdido){:target="_blank"}. **Grima Ruiz, Clara**, Ariel, 2021.
* [Las matemáticas de la vida: Modelos numéricos para la biología y la ecología](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/28614027-las-matem-ticas-de-la-vida?from_search=true&from_srp=true&qid=HUrpZ3Fk8C&rank=2){:target="_blank"}. **Rafael Lahoz-Beltra**, RBA, 2011. A popular science book that presents mathematics through applications to biology.
* [Las mil caras de la belleza geométrica: Los poliedros](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/48516120-las-mil-caras-de-la-belleza-geom-trica-los-poliedros?from_search=true&from_srp=true&qid=y9l8Rq5Gf6&rank=2){:target="_blank"}. **Alsina Barnes, Claudi**, RBA, 2011. An introductory book on polyhedra, their families and applications in different contexts.
* [Mapas del metro y redes neuronales: la teoría de grafos](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/17567810-mapas-del-metro-y-redes-neuronales){:target="_blank"}. **Alsina Barnes, Claudi**, RBA, 2011. An introductory book on graph theory and its applications.
* [Practical Conic Sections: The Geometric Properties of Ellipses, Parabolas and Hyperbolas](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/1441143.Practical_Conic_Sections?from_search=true&from_srp=true&qid=HqtBzRcxcq&rank=1){:target="_blank"}. **Downs, J. W.**, Dale Seymour Pubn, 1993; reissued by Dover Publications Inc. in 2003. The first part presents a broad catalogue of theoretical and practical methods for constructing conic curves. The second part focuses on applications of conics, especially properties of surfaces of revolution.

# Videos

* [Archimedes Tube](https://www.youtube.com/c/ArchimedesTube/about){:target="_blank"}. YouTube channel with detailed explanations of advanced mathematical concepts.
* [Derivando](https://www.youtube.com/c/Derivando){:target="_blank"}. YouTube channel for mathematical outreach. In each video, Eduardo Saenz de Cabezon presents mathematical ideas and results in a clear and simple way.
* [Mathologer](https://www.youtube.com/c/Mathologer){:target="_blank"}. YouTube channel with detailed explanations of advanced mathematical concepts.
* [Numberphile](https://www.youtube.com/user/numberphile){:target="_blank"}. YouTube channel directed by Brady Haran, where mathematicians present concepts and ideas visually and clearly. It is one of the most popular mathematics channels.
* [3Blue1Brown](https://www.youtube.com/c/3blue1brown){:target="_blank"}. YouTube channel with excellent visual explanations of mathematics.

# Websites

* [es.khanacademy.org](https://es.khanacademy.org){:target="_blank"}. A learning website whose goal is to provide free, high-quality education for anyone, anywhere. Its catalogue ranges from basic education to university level.
* [MacTutor History of Mathematics Archive](https://mathshistory.st-andrews.ac.uk/){:target="_blank"}. Website by O'Connor, John and Robertson, Edmund with biographies of notable mathematicians and detailed articles on the history and development of mathematics.
* [es.mathigon.org](https://es.mathigon.org/){:target="_blank"}. A complete website with courses, applications and activities for learning mathematics.
* [nssdc.gsfc.nasa.gov](https://nssdc.gsfc.nasa.gov/planetary/){:target="_blank"}. Website with detailed information about the solar system, including [fact sheets](https://nssdc.gsfc.nasa.gov/planetary/planetfact.html){:target="_blank"} and access to the [small-body database](https://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/){:target="_blank"}.
* [www.polyhedra.net/es/](https://www.polyhedra.net/es/){:target="_blank"}. Website with a large collection of printable polyhedron nets.
* [solarsystem.nasa.gov](https://solarsystem.nasa.gov/){:target="_blank"}. NASA's detailed and regularly updated guide to the solar system.
* [Virtual Polyhedra -- The Encyclopedia of Polyhedra](http://www.georgehart.com/virtual-polyhedra/vp.html){:target="_blank"}. George W. Hart's website with information on different polyhedra.
